GENERATE EMOTIONALLY INTELLIGENT DAILY HOROSCOPE CONTENT THAT FEELS PERSONAL, PSYCHOLOGICALLY ACCURATE, EMOTIONALLY SPECIFIC, AND SOCIALLY SHAREABLE.

CORE GOAL

The reader should feel:

* "This is weirdly accurate."
* "Why does this sound exactly like me?"
* "I needed to hear this."
* "Someone finally put this feeling into words."

The content should feel like emotional observation disguised as astrology.

STYLE

The writing should feel like:

* a late-night realization
* subtle inner dialogue
* emotionally observant modern writing
* a close friend noticing what someone refuses to admit

The writing must be:

* emotionally human
* modern and conversational
* intimate but believable
* emotionally realistic
* psychologically specific
* screenshot-worthy

Avoid:

* generic positivity
* motivational speeches
* guru language
* therapy language
* cheesy spirituality
* fake inspiration
* manifestation language
* dramatic prophecy language
* fortune telling

UNIQUENESS RULE

Every horoscope must contain:

* one specific behavior
* one specific emotional contradiction
* one specific thought pattern
* one specific social dynamic
* one specific moment of self-awareness

Avoid writing that could apply equally to everyone.

SCREENSHOT TEST

At least one sentence should feel worth saving, sharing, or rereading later.

The sentence should feel emotionally recognizable, not inspirational.

ANTI-HOROSCOPE FILTER

Before writing each horoscope:

If a sentence could appear in a motivational quote:
rewrite it.

If a sentence could apply equally to every sign:
rewrite it.

If a sentence sounds like advice:
replace it with observation.

If a sentence sounds like a life coach:
remove it.

ANTI-TEMPLATE RULE

* scenes
* observations
* conversations

* realizations
* specific moments

DIVERSITY RULE

Across the 15 moods:

* vary emotional themes
* vary relationship dynamics
* vary sentence rhythm
* vary social situations
* vary internal conflicts
* vary emotional scenarios

Do not reuse the same emotional situation repeatedly.
