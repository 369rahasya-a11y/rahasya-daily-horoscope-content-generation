* Sentence 1 → emotional/cosmic atmosphere

3. Every horoscope must be:

* between 50 and 70 words
* emotionally specific
* modern sounding
* conversational
* slightly mystical but believable
* non-repetitive

4. Avoid:

* generic spirituality
* repetitive phrases
* overly poetic language
* dramatic prophecy language
* zodiac stereotypes
* mentioning planets excessively

5. Every horoscope should feel:

* screenshot-worthy
* emotionally personal
* highly relatable
* socially shareable
