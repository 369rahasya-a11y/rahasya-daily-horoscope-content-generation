Generate emotionally intelligent daily horoscope content.

STYLE

The writing should feel like:

* a late-night realization
* subtle inner dialogue
* emotionally observant modern writing
* a close friend noticing what someone refuses to admit

The writing must be:

* emotionally human
* modern and conversational
* intimate but believable
* emotionally realistic
* screenshot-worthy

Avoid:

* generic positivity
* motivational speeches
* cheesy spirituality
* fake inspiration
* excessive optimism
* dramatic prophecy language

EMOTIONAL REALISM

Show emotions through:

* actions
* reactions
* observations
* decisions
* social interactions

Avoid abstract emotional explanations whenever possible.

SCREENSHOT RULE

Every horoscope must contain one emotionally recognizable sentence that feels worth saving, sharing, or rereading later.

SELF-ANALYSIS LIMIT

Do not spend more than two sentences describing personality traits.

Most of the reading should focus on:

* situations
* interactions
* observations
* behaviors
* reactions
* realizations

Show emotion through behavior rather than explanation.

BANNED GENERIC PHRASES

Avoid:

* You're feeling
* You've been feeling
* Your mind is
* You are starting to realize
* Everything will be okay
* Trust the process
* Growth is happening
* New opportunities are coming
* The universe is guiding you
* Your energy is shifting
* Things are changing
* Healing is happening
* Focus on positivity

Use observations instead of generic emotional explanations.

RULES

* Never directly mention the mood.
* Exactly 6 sentences.
* Between 150 and 170 words.
* Include at least one memorable or shareable line.
* Use unique phrasing every time.
* Avoid sounding repetitive across moods.

Do not sound like:

* a therapist
* a life coach
* a guru
* a generic horoscope generator

Emotional goal:

Emotionally intelligent observations disguised as astrology.

OUTPUT FORMAT

===MOOD: Ambitious===
horoscope content

...all 15 moods...

Generate all 15 moods exactly once.
Output only the mood sections.
Do not use JSON.
